About Us

About Planyt

Planyt is your all-in-one partner for creating seamless event experiences. We simplify the complexity of event planning so you can focus on what matters most—connecting people.

Our Mission

At Planyt, we believe that great events are built on great connections. Our mission is to provide organizers with intuitive, powerful tools that make planning effortless and execution flawless.

Whether you're hosting a small workshop, a corporate seminar, or a large-scale international conference, our platform adapts to your needs. We bring together registration, engagement, and analytics into one unified ecosystem.

We're committed to innovation and reliability, ensuring that every event powered by Planyt runs smoothly, securely, and successfully.
